TRUE ⇒ For the first time, computer used vacuum tube, an electronic device to hold computation.  (slide 8 first sentence)

4. Today’s processor is using transistor to replace vacuum tubes.
FALSE ⇒  The next generation is the use of Integrated Circuit (IC) to replace transistor (slide 9 first sentence)

The bamboo, then, is cut into tubes.To produce certain tones, the tube is cut and carved on its sides. Two to four tubes are then attached to a bamboo frame using rattan cords. Each angklung produces one single note, so several players must work together to play the music.The collaboration among the players is seen as the main valueof angklung.
