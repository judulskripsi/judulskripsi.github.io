Animasi stop motion (juga disebut animasi stop frame) adalah animasi yang ditangkap satu frame pada satu waktu, dengan objek fisik yang dipindahkan antar frame.

17. Buatlah MOVIE REVIEW (tema/judulnya bebas) seperti yang ada pada contoh di gambar. Harus sama percis seperti contohnya. Jangan copy paste, terimakasih.​


MOVIE UP

genre: Animation, Adventure, Comedy

release date : 29 Juli 2009

duration : 1 hour 36 minutes

director : Pete Doctor, Bob Peterson

Cast: Edward Asner (Carld Fredricksen), Jordan Nagai (Russel)

Movie review :

The film Up, which aired in 2019, is a film that tells the story of a grandfather and a child named Russell who are on an adventure to find Paradise Fall by flying a house using ten thousand balloons.

The story begins when a boy named Carl Fredricksen and his girlfriend Ellie both idolize an adventurous character named Charlez Muntz. As adults, Carl and Ellie married. Unfortunately they were not blessed with a child. Until finally Carl and Ellie decided to set aside some of their money for savings.

They aspire to find Paradise Fall and build a home there like their idol, Charlez Muntz. Unfortunately, this dream has not been realized even when Carl and Ellie are old. Until finally Ellie died. A lonely Carl refuses to move out of his house, despite a construction company offering to buy his house.

Long story short, Carls got into a fight with one of the workers who damaged his mailbox so Carls was sentenced to move house and had to live in a nursing home. Carls was disappointed, but he didn't lose his mind. He remembers his unfulfilled dream with Elly and decides to go on an adventure to find Paradise Fall.

Carls installed ten thousand helium gas balloons. The pressure from the balloons created cracks all over the bottom of the house and sent Carls' house flying. Grandpa was so happy, finally his dream with Elly will come true.

Suddenly there was a knock from inside Carls' room when the house was flying high. It turns out that a little boy named Russell will accompany his long journey. Their adventure begins. They experienced many incidents along the way until they finally made it to Paradise Fall and set up a house there.

Advantages

The advantage of this film is that the animation is very interesting with a typical character description. I think the idea of this film is so new that it will stick in my mind.

The film also includes humorous scenes that make the audience laugh and enjoy every adventure that is presented.

Deficiency

I found no flaws in this film. Because in my opinion, this film is successful in targeting the age of the audience, both small and adult. For example, like me, who watched it at the age of 9 when it was shown on TV, this film is really entertaining and it would be a shame to miss it

semoga bermanfaat

CMIIW
