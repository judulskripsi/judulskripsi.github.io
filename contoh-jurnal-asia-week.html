39. 16. How often does Rena practice the dance?A. Once a weekC.Three times a weekB. Twice a weekD.Four times a week​


Jawaban dari how often Rena practice the dance adalah three times a week. Arti dari pertanyaannya adalah "berapa kali Rena berlatih tari". Dari jadwal di bawah kita mengetahui bahwa Rena practices the dance three times a week.

Pembahasan

Perhatikan jadwal Rena dalam satu minggu (Rena's weekly schedule) berikut ini.

Monday

07.00 -13.30 School

15.00 - 17.00 Swimming

Tuesday

07.00 -13.30 School

15.00 - 17.00 Dancing Club

Wednesday

07.00 -13.30 School

15.00 - 17.00 Cinema

Thursday

07.00 - 13.30 School

14.00 - 15.00 Visit the library

Friday

07.00 - 11.00 School

15.00 - 17.00 Dancing Club

Saturday

09.00 -11.00 English course

15.00 - 17.00 Dancing Club

Dari jadwal di atas, kita mengetahui bahwa Rena berlatih tari pada hari Selasa (Tuesday), Jumat (Friday), dan Sabtu (Saturday). Ini berarti Rena practice the dance three times a week (C).
