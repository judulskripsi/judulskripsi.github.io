Jessica: Annie, do you have idea to spend your Summer holiday this year?

Annie: Not yet, Jess. Do you have any plan already? Share it with me.

Jessica: Yes, I am planning to go to Bali with my sister. But yesterday she said that she is having problem with taking leave from her job. So my mother was suggesting maybe I should come with you to Bali. What do you say?

Annie: Sounds great! I have never been to Bali before. What are we going to do there?

Jessica: I want to visit Beach Clubs that is popular in Bali and learning how to surf. It would be great to travel with you there.

Annie: Alright then. What about creating our itinerary tomorrow? It wil be more fun if we discuss in together.

Jessica: Let's do it, then!
