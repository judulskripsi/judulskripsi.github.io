8. Sebutkan Contoh dan kegunaanya : 1. Karet Alami 2. Karet Kimiawi 3. Karet Sintetis


Karet alam memiliki banyak sifat
unggul. Karet alam memiliki daya elastis
atau daya lenting yang baik, plastisitas
yang baik, mudah pengolahannya, tidak
mudah aus (tidak mudah habis karena
gesekan), dan tidak mudah panas. Sifat
unggul lain dari karet alami adalah memiliki daya tahan yang tinggi terhadap
keretakan, tahan hentakan yang berulang-ulang, serta daya lengket yang
tinggi terhadap berbagai bahan. Dalam bidang industri, karet alam memiliki
peran yang sangat besar. Contohnya adalah ban pesawat terbang dan ban
mobil balap dibuat dari bahan baku utama karet alam murni.
Karet sintetis terbuat dari bahan baku yang berasal dari minyak bumi,
batu bara, minyak, gas alam, dan acetylene. Banyak dari karet sintetis
adalah kopolimer, yaitu polimer yang terdiri dari lebih dari satu jenis
monomer. Karet sintetis dapat diubah susunannya sehingga diperoleh sifat
yang sesuai dengan kegunaannya. Karet sintetis dapat digunakan untuk
berbagai keperluan, bahkan dapat menggantikan fungsi karet alam. Karet
sintetis mempunyai beberapa keunggulan dibanding karet alam yaitu tahan
terhadap suhu tinggi/panas, minyak, pengaruh udara, dan kedap gas. Karet
sintetis memiliki banyak jenis.
Secara kimiawi karet alam adalah
senyawa hidrokarbon yang merupakan
polimer alam hasil penggumpalan lateks
alam dan merupakan makromolekul
poliisoprena (C5H8)n.

jawaban

A. - sifat kaca = tembus pandang/ transparan, mudah pecah, dan dapat meleleh pada suhu tinggi dan komposisi utama dalam yang kebanyakan dipakai dalam pembuatan kaca ialah silika.

- contoh kaca = kaca cermin, kaca warna, kaca patri, kaca bening, kaca laminasi, kaca es

B. - sifat logam = Logam adalah unsur yang memiliki sifat khas yaitu mengkilat atau berkilau, keras, memiliki kepadatan tinggi, dapat diregangkan dan ditempa, memiliki titik leleh dan titik didih yang tinggi, serta merupakan konduktor panas dan listrik yang baik.

- contoh logam = aluminium, tembaga, emas, besi, timah, perak, titanium, uranium, dan seng.

C. - sifat karet = elastisitas dan daya lentur yang baik, plastis, tidak mudah panas, dan tidak mudah retak.

- contoh karet = silicone, styrene butadiene rubber, butadiene nitrile rubber, viton, dll

Jawaban:

Once upon a time, my friends and I decided to embark on a memorable holiday adventure to the rustic village of Kampoeng Karet Ngargoyoso. We had heard tales of its pristine natural beauty and the warm hospitality of its inhabitants, and we couldn't resist the temptation to explore it.

Our journey began with a scenic train ride that took us through lush green landscapes and picturesque rice fields. When we arrived at the village, we were greeted with open arms by the friendly villagers. They had prepared traditional Javanese cuisine for us, and we indulged in delicious dishes like Nasi Liwet and Gudeg.

During our stay, we explored the village's rubber plantations, where we learned about the rubber-making process. We even tried our hand at tapping rubber trees, an experience that gave us a newfound appreciation for this essential natural resource.

One sunny morning, we set out on a trek to the nearby waterfall, guided by a local villager. The hike was challenging but rewarding, as we were treated to the breathtaking sight of a pristine waterfall cascading into a crystal-clear pool. We couldn't resist taking a refreshing dip in the cool waters.

In the evenings, we gathered around a bonfire, where the villagers entertained us with traditional Javanese music and dance performances. We joined in the festivities, learning to dance to the rhythmic beat of the gamelan and trying our best at Javanese dance moves.

As our time in Kampoeng Karet Ngargoyoso came to an end, we couldn't help but feel a deep connection to this charming village. The simplicity of life, the natural beauty, and the warmth of the people had left an indelible mark on our hearts. With heavy hearts, we bid farewell, promising to return one day to relive the enchanting memories of our holiday in this charming rubber village.

40. buatlah cerita pengalaman liburan di wisata kampoeng karet ngargoyoso bersama keluarga menggunakan bahasa inggris verb 2 ​


Jawaban:

Title: A Memorable Vacation at Kampoeng Karet Ngargoyoso

Last summer, my family and I had an unforgettable vacation at Kampoeng Karet Ngargoyoso. It was a thrilling adventure filled with exciting activities and beautiful natural surroundings. We were thrilled to experience the charm of the countryside and spend quality time together as a family.

Upon arriving at Kampoeng Karet Ngargoyoso, we were warmly welcomed by the friendly staff. They provided us with a cozy cottage surrounded by lush greenery. The cottage had a rustic feel and offered a serene view of the nearby rolling hills.

The first day of our vacation was spent exploring the breathtaking landscapes of the area. We went on a nature walk through the rubber plantation, breathing in the fresh air and admiring the tall rubber trees. The guide shared interesting facts about rubber production, and we even got to witness the process of latex extraction.

The next day, we joined a thrilling river rafting adventure along the crystal-clear river that ran through the village. We glided through gentle rapids and laughed together as we got splashed by the refreshing water. It was an exhilarating experience that left us with beautiful memories and plenty of laughs.

One of the highlights of our trip was the opportunity to learn about the traditional process of making rubber. We visited a rubber factory and observed the skilled workers as they transformed raw rubber into various products. We were amazed by their craftsmanship and dedication.

In the evenings, we gathered around a bonfire and enjoyed a delightful cultural performance by the local villagers. They showcased traditional dances and music, and even invited us to participate. It was a joyous celebration of the local culture, and we felt privileged to be part of it.

During our stay, we also indulged in delicious local cuisine. We savored traditional Javanese dishes, such as Gudeg, Sate Ayam, and Bakso, which were bursting with flavors. We even had the chance to try our hand at cooking some of these dishes with the guidance of the talented local cooks.

As our vacation came to an end, we reflected on the amazing time we had at Kampoeng Karet Ngargoyoso. The experience brought us closer as a family, and we were grateful for the opportunity to immerse ourselves in the natural beauty and rich cultural heritage of the village.

Leaving Kampoeng Karet Ngargoyoso, we carried with us fond memories, vibrant photographs, and a renewed appreciation for nature and family bonds. It was a truly remarkable vacation that we would cherish forever.

Note: The verb 2 (past tense) forms used in the story include: welcomed, provided, spent, went, breathed, admired, shared, witnessed, glided, laughed, left, gathered, enjoyed, showcased, invited, participated, savored, tried, and reflected.
