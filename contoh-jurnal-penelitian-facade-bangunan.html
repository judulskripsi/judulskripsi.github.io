Reading Comprehension

Reading comprehension adalah kemampuan untuk memahami dan memproses sesuatu yang kita baca. Dalam reading comprehension kalian perlu membaca text tersebut secara seksama dan berulang-ulang untuk bisa memahami inti dari text yang di baca.

Pembahasan

1.What is the purpose of the letter above?

Answer : The purpose of this letter is to give some advice to Chris regarding his problems.

2.What are the problems faced by the receiver of the letter?

Answer : The letter's receiver who is Chris faced many problems, first he has a problem with his examination, he is worried about his examination and how to prepare for it, secondly, his parents think he is lazy and irresponsible, third, he also got a problem with one of his best friends, Jane.

3.Why do Chris's parents think that he is lazy and irresponsible?

Answer : I think because he does not get a good result on his study or examinations.

4.What are the suggestions suggested by Jacky?

Answer : There are many suggestions that Jacky offers to Chris :

For his examination preparation, he suggested that Chris should work hard on his study and try to ask the teacher if he doesn't understand. Beside study hard he also should relax like listening to quiet music and have some exercises.Chris should talk to his parents about his problem.Chris should care and give more time to Jane, Jane is one of Chris's best friends.Last, he also cheers Chris up by saying for every problem, there must be a way out!

5....your mother to find a housemate to help you? (paragraph 3)

Answer : I can't find this sentence in paragraph 3. Mungkin ada kesalahan soal?
