8. Contoh latihan kebugaran AUD


Contoh latihan kebugaran untuk anak usia dini antara lain adalah berenang, sepakbola, senam ringan, inline skate, bersepeda dan lain sebagainya.

Pembahasan

Haloo adik adik yang ganteng ganteng dan cantik cantik balik lagi nih sama kakak kece. Gimana kabar kalian? Insyaallah sehat sehat terus yaa. Kali ini kakak a1m akan mencoba membantu menjawab pertanyaan ini ya, kuy deh dibaca pelan pelan biar masuk hati.

Perlu diketahui bahwa badan sehat dan kebugaran anak usia dini dapat mempengaruhi tumbuhkembangnya anak tersebut secara optimal. Secara fisik yang dapat dikembangkan ialah pada motorik kasar dan halusnya, secara sosial dan emosionalnya dapat dilatih pada aspek kognitifnya. Latihan kebugaran pada anak usia dini dapat melatiha kekuata otot dan tulang anak tersebut agar lebih kuat, sehingga anak-anak perlu diberika latihan kebugaran yang meliputi pemanasan, latihan dan pendinginan.

Contoh latihan kebugaran untuk anak usia dini antara lain:

1. Berenang

Dalam latihan berenang salah satu latihan yang dianjurka dikarenakan melalui latihan ini anak akan memiliki kemampuan dalam air, ia dapat merasakan ketenangan dalam air, memecahka masalah dalam air dan bersahabat dengan air. Dalam hal ini pernapasannya akan terlatih dan juga dapat melatih kerja otot tubuh juga. Harapannya dengan berenang anak akan terhindar dari penyakit pernapasan seperti asma.

2. Sepakbola

Dalam latihan sepakbola akan memperkuat otot, fleksibilitas, daya tahan kardiovaskularnya dan pembentukan koodinasi pada mata dan kaki. Mentalnya pun akan terlatih menjadi lebih sportif dan disiplin juga melatih kerjasama.

3. Senam Ringan

Dalam latihan senam ringan dapat melatih kelenturan dan keseimbangan tubuhnya sehingga tidak mudah terjatuh.

4. Inline Skate

Dalam latihan inline skate berfungsi untuk melatih kesehatan jantung, meningkatkan keseimbangan tubuh, tubuh juga dapat menjadi lentur serta melatih koordinasi dan kekuatan otot.

5. Bersepeda

dalam latihan bersepeda latihannya memiliki manfaat yang hampir sama dengan inline skate yaitu menjaga kesehatan jantung, melatih kelenturan dan kekuatan otot-otor serta koordinasinya.

Demikian lah penjabar dari kakak, semoga dengan ilmu yang sedikiti ini dapat bermanfaat untuk adik adik semua.

20. Selisih usia audi dan bima 5 tahun. Bima lebih muda daripada audi. Hasil kuadrat usia bima sama dengan 1 lebih dari empat kali usia audi. Tentukan usia audi


Audi = A
Bima = B

a - b = 5 --> b = a - 5

b² = 4a + 1
(a - 5)² = 4a + 1
a² - 10a + 25 = 4a + 1
a² - 10a - 4a + 25 - 1 = 0
a² - 14a + 24 = 0
(a - 12) (a - 2) = 0

a = 12 tahun atau a = 2 tahun,
Jika a = 2 tahun, b = -3 tahun. TIDAK MUNGKIN
Jika a = 12 tahun, b = 7 tahun MUNGKIN

Jadi, usia Audi = 12 tahun.

B-A=15

B-6A=-25

5A=40

A=8

B=15+8

40. sekripsi tentang gunung bromo dengan hbs inggris​


Jawaban:

Seeing the beauty of the sun rising from the eastern horizon on Mount Bromo slowly might be an endless grace. The combination of yellow, orange, black and blue produced by natural phenomena is truly an interesting sight that is presented to the eyes of those who see it.

The beauty of Mount Bromo which is in the Mount Semeru Region is already well known to foreign countries. This mountain is considered sacred by the Tengger people, a tribe that inhabits the Mount Bromo region. The name Bromo itself is taken from the name of the main deity of the Hindu people, namely Brahma.

Take a trip to Mount Bromo, our feet will be welcomed by a sand crater that runs along the area of one of the mountains in East Java. When we travel to Bromo in the morning, we will be presented with beautiful colors derived from sand exposed to sunlight. The sand here also seemed to whisper when swept away by the breeze that blows calm when the morning greets.

While walking, we can see several buildings that stand around this area. One of them is the Poten building. This sturdy building that stands in the middle of the sea of sand becomes a place of worship used by the Tengger Tribe community. Balinese Hindu-style architecture is very attached to this building. Every doorway is guarded by a statue that looks like a lion. These statues are intended to ward off evil spirits who will disturb the Mount Bromo area.

Not only a tourist attraction, Mount Bromo is also a place for Hindu religious rituals. One of the rituals held by the Tengger Tribe here every year is the Yadnya Kasada ritual. In this ritual, the Tengger Tribe Society presents offerings in the form of livestock and food for the gods who inhabit mountains in the region such as Mount Semeru, Batok, Bromo and Pananjakan.

Being in the Mount Bromo Region and witnessing the natural beauty presented here has indeed been an unforgettable experience. Exotic sand, a beautiful sunrise and interaction with the Tengger tribe certainly make the trip through the Mount Bromo area feel so enjoyable.

Jawaban:

Mount Bromo (Indonesian, Gunung Bromo), is an active volcano and part of the Tengger massif, in East Java, Indonesia. At 2,329 meters (7,641 ft) it is not the highest peak of the massif, but is the best known. The massif area is one of the most visited tourist attractions in East Java, Indonesia. The volcano belongs to the Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park. The name of Bromo derived from Javanese pronunciation of Brahma, the Hindu creator god.

Mount Bromo sits in the middle of a plain called the "Sea of Sand" (Javanese, Segara Wedi or Indonesian, Lautan Pasir), a protected nature reserve since 1919. The typical way to visit Mount Bromo is from the nearby mountain village of Cemoro Lawang. From there it is possible to walk to the volcano in about 45 minutes, but it is also possible to take an organised jeep tour, which includes a stop at the viewpoint on Mount Penanjakan (2,770 m or 9,088 ft) (Indonesian: Gunung Penanjakan). The viewpoint on Mount Penanjakan can also be reached on foot in about two hours.

Depending on the degree of volcanic activity, the Indonesian Centre for Volcanology and Disaster Hazard Mitigation sometimes issues warnings against visiting Mount Bromo.
