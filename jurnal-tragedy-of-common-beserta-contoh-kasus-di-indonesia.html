5. can you describe the tecnology of the tragedy?​


As technology advances, we become more adept at exploiting our environment. As our understanding of institutions advances, we become more adept at avoiding over exploitation. However, many people believe our technology is outpacing our institutions. We are still creating new episodes of the tragedy of the commons (Hardin, 1968). The purpose of this study is to understand technological change and the exploitation of the commons. As an example of the commons, a dynamic model of an ocean fishery is generalised to include consumer and producer benefits and technological advances in catching fish. Then the model is applied to a fishery with primitive technology and to a fishery with advancing technology over time. Results are compared for open access with no institutions and optimal management with efficient institutions. With primitive technology, institutions for the commons are irrelevant. The commons can be sustainably and beneficially managed without them. Technological advances require institutional advances. New technology without new institutions will crash the commons and beggar the community that exploits it. However new technology with new institutions can sustain the commons and enrich the community.

11. rumus cost of common equity


rumus biaya ekuitas yang terjadi
Ke = Rf + (Rm-Rf) E

Ke = cost of equity / biaya modal sendiri
RF = risk free rate / tingkat bunga bebas resiko
Rm=tingkat pengembalian pasar
E adalah betaEkonomi
Komponen Biaya
Biaya Ekuitas



Biaya ekuitas merupakan tingkat pengembalian yang diharapkan oleh investor ketika para investor menginvestasikan uangnya.

Rumus Biaya Ekuitas:

1. Biaya Ekuitas untuk Saham Biasa (Common Stock)
    RS = D1: (P0 – f) + g

    Note:
    RS = Biaya ekuitas saham biasa
    D1 = Dividen yang akan dibayarkan pada akhir tahun (dalam Rp)
    P0 = Harga Saham saat ini (dalam Rp)
    f    = Flotation Cost/ biaya  akibat penerbitan saham baru
    g   = Pertumbuhan dividen (dalam %)


2. Rumus Biaya Ekuitas Saham Preferen
    Rp = Dp ÷ (Pp – f)

    Note:
    Rp = Biaya ekuitas saham biasa
    Dp = Dividen yang dibayarkan tiap tahun
    P0 = Harga Saham preferen saat ini (dalam Rp)
    f    = Flotation Cost/ biaya  akibat penerbitan saham baru


3. Rumus Biaya Ekuitas dengan Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM)
    Ke = Rf + (Rm - Rf) E

    Note:
    Ke  = Cost of Equity/ biaya modal sendiri
    Rf   = Risk Free Rate/ tingkat bunga bebas resiko
    Rm = Tingkat pengembalian pasar
    E    = Beta

Aristotle suggested that poetry was composed of comedy, tragedy, and epic

.

when you want to write article/journal/research paper, you have to read a lot of books as references and you may take the notes of the expert's opinion you must remember the rules to avoid plagiarism:

1. if you take all the the expert's opinion without adding your idea, you must give a quotation mark (".."), the year and page. For example: "Poetry was composed of comedy, tragedy, and epic" (Aristotle, 1880:24)

.

2. if you just take the main point of the expert's idea and mixing with your idea, no need to put in ".." For example: Approve to Aristotle we know that poetry is an art of comedy, tragedy, and epic which contains a very poetic choice of words. or you can write like this one: Aristotle suggested that poetry was composed of comedy, tragedy, and epic (remember to use simple past tense form if you take the opinion with your fewer idea)

Aristotle suggested that..

Alexander said that..

Soekarno agreed that..

30. apa definisi dari proper nouns,common nouns,compound nouns beserta contoh nya​


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

Proper nouns : kata benda, tempat, maupun orang yang disebutkan secara umum.

Contoh : building, river, book, people, etc.

Contoh kalimat : They swim in the river.

Common nouns : kata benda, tempat, maupun orang yang disebutkan secara spesifik.

Contoh : building => the Great Wall of China, river => Ciliwung river, book => Mathematics book, people => Joko Widodo, etc.

Contoh kalimat : They swim in the Ciliwung river.

Compound nouns : kata benda yang terbentuk dari dua atau lebih kata dan memiliki makna baru. Kata-kata tersebut bisa dari kombinasi :

- Noun + noun

 Contoh : newspaper

- Noun + verb

 Contoh : waterfall

- Noun + Adjective

 Contoh : snow white

- Verb + Noun

 Contoh : swimming pool

- Adjective + Noun

 Contoh : whiteboard

- etc
