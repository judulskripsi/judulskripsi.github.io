Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

a. Heading adalah bagian yang menunjukkan nama dan alamat pengirim surat.

b. Inside address adalah bagian yang menunjukkan nama dan alamat penerima surat.

c. Salutation adalah bagian yang berisi salam pembuka yang sopan dan sesuai dengan etika berkomunikasi, seperti "Dear Mr./Mrs./Ms. [nama]".

d. Enclosure adalah bagian yang menunjukkan bahwa surat tersebut menyertakan lampiran.

e. Reference line adalah bagian yang menunjukkan topik atau judul surat yang ditulis oleh pengirim.

penjelasan :

Generic Structure of Personal Letter:

1. Date : Date when the letter is writen (top left).

2. Address : Place where you are writing fron (top right).

3. Salutation and Name : Greeting and the person's name you are writing to.

4. Introduction : The opening of the letter usually starts with "how are you?" or refers to previous letter.

5. Body : The main part of the letter. It includes what you want to write to the other person.

6. Closure : The part indicates the letter is going to end.

7. Complimentary close : Short expressions like "love you", "sincerely yours”, "love".

8. Signature : Signature or initials of the writer.

9. Postscript P.S : After thougt in a letter. You begin with P.S. and end it with your initials.

Club of a Foreign Mission

Islamabad

September 7rd, 2012

Application for Assistant Accountant

Dear Hiring Manager,

I was excited to discover that you are seeking to fulfill the Assistant Accountant position at Club of a Foreign Mission as advertised recently on Newspaper, 3rd September 2012.

Based on the requirements said in the ad, I believe that I’m qualified enough to your requirements. I possess lots of experience, knowledge, and skills that would be a great contribute to your company.

I can offer you my experience, skills, and abilities which include as follow:

3,5 years experience as assistant accountant staff.Awarded Employee of the Year for exceeding all assigned targets.Reducing unecessary accounting cost by 25% within one year.

I am also a holder of a bachelor's degree in Finance from University of Islamabad. At the university, I was involved in multiple clubs and societies. In addition to being a native English speaker, I am adept at using all software programs necessary the role, including MS Office and MYOB.

The enclosed resume would tell more details on the above, as well as my background and profile included.

I’m expecting you to review my resume, and would be pleased to wait an opportunity to discuss your company’s goals and talk to you about the value that I can bring to Club of a Foreign Mission. See you soon!

Sincerely,

Marielle Ranae.

Pembahasan:

Applications letter (dalam bahasa Indonesia: surat lamaran kerja) is a letter that you write to a company when you are applying for a job. Surat lamaran kerja adalah surat resmi yang dibuat oleh seseorang untuk melamar pekerjaan. Surat lamaran pekerjaan ini berisi tentang latar belakang dan minat pelamar pada lowongan pekerjaan yang ditawarkan.

Struktur Applications Letter

Kepala surat (alamat surat, alamat pengirim dan tanggal surat).Salam pembuka.Isi surat.Penutup.
