ASKING AND GIVING DIRECTION

Percakapan tentang asking and giving direction biasa kita lakukan dalam kehidupan sehari-hari? Apa itu asking and giving direction? Merupakan percakapan untuk menanyakan arah ke suatu tempat yang akan kita tuju.

Ada beberapa ekspresi yang biasanya kita gunakan dalam membuat kalimat asking and giving direction.

Ekspresi asking direction :

- Could you tell me the way to....?

- Can you show the way to...?

- Can you direct me to...?

- Excuse, do you know the way to...?

Ekspresi giving direction :

- Go straight ahead

- Turn left/turn right

- Take bus number 23 and get off in the next bus stop

- It's only two block away from here

Let's complete the following conversation with the correct words
